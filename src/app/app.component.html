Vortex

Vortex is an advanced Inter-carrier Settlement Solution and CDR reconciliation tool designed for telecommunications companies. It streamlines the settlement process between carriers and ensures accurate reconciliation of Call Detail Records (CDRs), enhancing operational efficiency and reducing discrepancies.

Gauge

A CDR reconciliation software designed to address the challenges of identifying, eliminating, and preventing the termination of illegal/grey traffic in Pakistan by various LDI operators. It processes operator traffic to highlight IPs and provides analysis on calls terminated in the network. Consequently, the system generates traffic reports to assist users in making informed policy decisions.

Boink

Boink is a hybrid mobile application designed for restaurant reservations, online food ordering, and taxi booking services. The app provides a seamless user experience by integrating multiple functionalities into a single platform, allowing users to easily book a table at their favorite restaurant, order food for delivery or pickup, and arrange transportation to the restaurant. The application leverages modern technologies to ensure a smooth and efficient user experience.
